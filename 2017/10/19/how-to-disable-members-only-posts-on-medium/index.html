How to disable members-only posts on Medium

Published: Thu 19 October 2017
By frank

In Uncategorized.

I really like Medium, it's a beautiful website with lots of interesting pieces to read. But I don't get that it raised 132 million dollars in VC funding. That's a big pile of money, that the VCs expect to earn back eightfold in a couple of years. This raises concern that Medium will have to milk every penny out of their website, inevitably decreasing the user experience. Read more about this on Signal v. Noise.

Medium recently introduced a paid membership for \$ 5 per month. Now I'm not complaining about this, actually I think it's a nice model. It works well with for example De Correspondent. But what is annoying is that the members-only posts are mixed in with the free posts, effectively making the frontpage a minefield for non-paying users such as myself.

Luckily there are two fixes. The first is to get a paid membership. The second involves a custom filter in your adblocking program. Here are the lines you need to add:

medium.com##.js-trackedPost:if(span.svgIcon--star)
